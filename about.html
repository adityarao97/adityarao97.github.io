What We Do

Spring Socials is an event planning and managing company which deals in classic weddings to elegant corporate events and everything in between.

We are a team of highly dedicated and passionate individuals working hard to make your dream events come true. We take your problems as opportunities and bring the best out of it to give you a hassle free service.

At Spring Socials, we get into the minute details of every project regardless of its nature and scope. We treat your events as our own and understand the emotions attached to it. We give it all for you so that your events are completed on time and in the budget.




Our Team

Ritu Raj, founder and CEO of the company, has a different story altogether of how this company was formed. Quite intriguing it is. Started as an electronics engineer, but his interest in planning events brought him to college social groups where he planned and executed numerous events and eventually ended up at this versatile field of event management. He says, while he was exploring the field, he deeply discovered himself and his notion of life. In his quest to see joy in people's eyes, he took the bold step to start this company. He says, maybe not today, but by helping people find their happiness, someday he would know what contentment feels like and would find his own true happiness.

The team is made up of several other enthusiastic individuals who put in their passion and creativity in this field to serve you with best of services. The team is well organized and divided into subcategories which look after each and every requirement of the project. The work is carried in stages to ensure amazing outcomes. Any great event takes a lot and we at spring socials give it all. Your satisfaction is our topmost priority.
