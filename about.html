What We Do

Spring Socials is an event planning and managing company which organizes many different kinds of events ranging from classic weddings to elegant corporate events and everything in between.

We are a team of highly dedicated and passionate individuals working hard to make your dream events come true. The team not just executes the events, but also works closely with the clients to help them make the right decisions and choose innovative solutions. Give us your problems and we'll bring the best out of them to give you a hassle free experience.

At Spring Socials, every aspect of an event is scrutinized and all minute details are looked upon. We treat your events like ours and understand the emotions attached to each of them. Our broad networking with vendors allows us to offer you customised choices. Our smart budget plans allow you to track your costs at all times. We give it all for you so that your events are completed on time and in the budget.

Our Vision

We have a very clear vision. We want to end the notion that people can be fooled by the monopoly of substandard service providers. We aim to become the organization that gives the most value to it's clients in the market. Availing services must be made a very simple and joyful experience.

Our Motto

We strive to deliver quality services and innovative solutions to our clients with total transparency. The goal is to make events more than special by adding to them flavours of fun, creativity and technology. To bring your desires to life, the team shall never hesitate to go the extra mile.

Our Team

Ritu Raj, founder and CEO of the company, has a different story altogether of how this company was formed. Quite intriguing it is. Started as an electronics engineer, but his interest in planning events brought him to college social groups where he planned and executed numerous events and eventually ended up at this versatile field of event management. He says, while he was exploring the field, he deeply discovered himself and his notion of life. In his quest to see joy in people's eyes, he took the bold step to start this company. He says, maybe not today, but by helping people find their happiness, someday he would know what contentment feels like and would find his own true happiness.

The team is made up of several other young, creative, dynamic and enthusiastic professionals who put in their passion and dedication in this field to serve you with best of services. The team is well organized and divided into sub categories which look after each and every requirement of the project. The work is carried in stages to ensure amazing outcomes. In addition to this, we have a wide range of quality vendors who helps us to provide outstanding services to our clients. Any event takes a lot of hard work and we at Spring Socials give it all. Your satisfaction is our topmost priority.
